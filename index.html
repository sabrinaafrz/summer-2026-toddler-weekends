How to add these to your calendar
Everything at once: click Download all (.ics), then open the file. On a Mac it imports into Apple Calendar; in Google Calendar go to Settings → Import & export → Import and choose the .ics file.
One weekend at a time: use the Add to Google Calendar button on any card.
Each event is an all-weekend (Sat–Sun) all-day entry, so it shows up across the whole weekend.
